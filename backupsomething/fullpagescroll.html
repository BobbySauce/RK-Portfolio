Welcome to the Zoomable Webpage

This entire page can be zoomed in and out using click and drag. Try dragging to the right to zoom in on any part of the page, or drag left to zoom out. Everything scales together - text, images, and layout.

How It Works

The zoom functionality transforms the entire page content as a single unit. When you click and drag, the zoom focuses on the point where you initially clicked, creating a natural zooming experience that keeps your point of interest centered.

You can also use the buttons in the top-right corner or keyboard shortcuts (+ to zoom in, - to zoom out, 0 or R to reset) for quick zoom control.
